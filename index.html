Fancy lobby

In the last decade, the hotel lobby has transformed to become a centrepiece of the guest experience. The floor was tiled in fine marble, which made every step echo. A chandelier made rainbow colors dance across the luxurious lobby. Embroidered silk sofas surrounded a large, flat-screen television. The twin doors that led into the lobby were a pristine white with golden handles. The desk was made of amber-colored wood and a green granite top. Exquisite paintings hung from the rich, red walls. Even the door hinges were engraved with swirls and elegant designs. Our lobby is the most luxurious and welcoming and will make you feel right at home.

ABOUT US

Palladium Hotel, built around 1690, captures the essence of an American country home. A place to delight, its elegant rooms have been restored and reimagined, while its unique setting inspires our garden-to-table dining and garden-scented Spa.Get together in the Bar or Croquet Room, choosing from the collection of wines in our vaulted cellars below, as well as our very own cyder.
